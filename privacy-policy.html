Privacy Policy

StudyShift App

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to StudyShift. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the StudyShift mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address (if you choose to create an account)
  • Study Data: Study sessions, shift timings, subjects studied, revision schedules, and progress tracking
  • App Settings: Daily study goals, target exam dates, notification preferences, and theme settings
  • User Generated Content: Study notes, reminders, and personal study plans

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, time spent in app, and crash reports
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics and technical diagnostic data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Core App Functionality: To track your study sessions, manage revision schedules, and provide progress analytics
  • Personalization: To customize your study experience and provide relevant insights
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app features and performance
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Backup & Sync: To enable cloud synchronization of your study data across devices (if enabled)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your study data is primarily stored locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms. This ensures your data remains private and accessible even without an internet connection.

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored securely on our cloud servers. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data during transmission and storage.

4.3 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to user data by authorized personnel only

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting providers)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Safety: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access and Control

  • Access: You can view all your study data within the app
  • Export: You can export your study data at any time through the app settings
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data through the app
  • Opt-out: You can disable cloud sync and keep all data local to your device

6.2 Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the StudyShift service. When you delete your account, we permanently delete your data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain certain information.

7. Children's Privacy

StudyShift is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

8.1 Google Authentication

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive only your basic profile information (name and email) for authentication purposes. We do not access your Google account data beyond what is necessary for sign-in.

8.2 Analytics

We may use anonymized analytics services to understand how our app is used. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be highlighted within the app.
